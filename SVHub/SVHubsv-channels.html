SpookVooper discord server channel guide:
Welcome
#rules: break these to get #faq: information for new people #vooperian-history: vooperian lore #fanmail: instructions on how to mail something to Spike's PO Box #welcome: documentation of incoming members
Announcements
#vooperio-feed: updates to Vooper.io #.announcements: where announcements about stuff happens #development-feed: Github things #roadmap: what Spike want's to do with VoopAI #sv-developer: Things for nerds (what you're allowed to do with the SpookVooper API) #nerdcraft-announcements: announcement about Spike's minecraft server #video-stream-feed: new videos #.news: stuff for news reporters to post their things
General Discussion
#.general: chat for miscellaneous discussion (PG; everywhere else is PG-14) #offtopic: general 2 #current-events: political discussion; (do -role CE to get in here) #memes: a place to send your "funnies" #venting: serious discussion; (do -role venting to get in here) #spikes-code-corner: nerd talk #nerdcraft: a place to discuss NerdCraft #creations: a place to share things you've made (e.g. art) #debate: go here if you want to debate (do -role debate to get in here) #.bots: use bots here (try not to spam) #.news-media: basically #.news but for the public #vc-text: for if you dont feel like talking; and music bots
Economy
#.economy-feed: live stock prices and trade feed #auctioneering: used to auction off things in NerdCraft #stock-talk: talk about stocks and advertise businesses #casino: gambling is bad; but we use fake money so who cares #forum-feed: new posts on SpookVooper.com's forum (edited)

Gaming
